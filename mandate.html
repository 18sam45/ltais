MANDATE



The LEGISLATIVE TECHNICAL AFFIARS AND INFORMATION SERIVCE (LTAIS) directs, oversees and reviews all works and activities performed by the Public Information, Publication and Media Relations Division (PIPMRD),Legislative Reference Division (LRefD),andTranslation and Interpretation Division (TID) in aid of the legislative work of the Members of Parliament and their respective staff and other officers of the Parliament.

It shall likewise take on the role of providing sources and resources to the visitors of Parliament, explaining what parliament is and how it works, To promote deeper public engagement, it may conduct outreach activities and hosting engagements.



VISION


A premier arm in providing information services to the public on all matters relating to the Bangsamoro Parliament.



MISSION


To continuously provide the public and other stakeholders essential information pertaining to the legislative affairs in the Bangsamoro Parliament through information resources and communication services for a well-informed citizenry.
